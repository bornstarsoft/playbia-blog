The Daily Mail has just launched Relink, a captivating new puzzle game that promises to engage your lateral thinking skills like never before. With its unique format of identifying imposters among a grid of words, Relink not only tests your cognitive abilities but also encourages creative problem-solving. Each puzzle is designed to challenge players to connect the dots in unexpected ways, making it a perfect fit for tech-savvy individuals looking for a mental workout.

Relink's gameplay mechanics are straightforward yet stimulating. Players must identify four imposters hidden within a grid of 16 words, each linked by a common theme. This blend of logic and wordplay makes Relink a daily ritual for those who enjoy a good brain teaser. Plus, with new puzzles released every day at midnight, it keeps the excitement alive and encourages friendly competition among friends.
