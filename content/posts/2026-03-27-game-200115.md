In an exciting development for the gaming world, the debut title from Vivix, "Artificial Detective," introduces players to a dystopian universe where a robot detective investigates the mysterious disappearance of humanity. This innovative game, revealed during an Xbox Partner Preview, showcases the talents of a team comprised of veterans from the acclaimed Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2, promising a unique blend of action and adventure.

Set in a city overrun by rogue robots, players will navigate through a complex narrative while leading an underdog team that includes a military robodog and a human girl. The game's premise revolves around uncovering the fate of humans who were once meant to be protected by machines that have since gone rogue. With stunning visuals and an engaging storyline, "Artificial Detective" is poised to captivate gamers and tech enthusiasts alike.

As we look forward to its release on Xbox Series X|S and PC, one can't help but wonder: will this game redefine how we perceive AI in gaming, or is it just another step in the evolution of interactive storytelling?
