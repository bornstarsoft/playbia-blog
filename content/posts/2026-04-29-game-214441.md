Get ready to embark on an exciting oceanic adventure with "Quack Quack Attack - PEGG Blaster," a new mobile puzzle game from HAOPLAY. Scheduled for release on iOS and Android later this year, this game invites players to take on the role of Captain Quack, navigating a magical ark while battling monsters and overcoming obstacles. The unique gameplay involves managing a squad of birds and strategically firing eggs to eliminate foes, making it a fresh addition to the casual puzzle genre.

With a roster of up to fifty unique birds across six classes, players can experiment with different combinations to optimize their strategies. The game also offers various modes, including the challenging Purgatory Tower and competitive Starry Expedition, ensuring there's something for everyone. Plus, pre-registration is now open, allowing players to secure exclusive rewards ahead of the official launch.
