As we gear up for 2026, SEGA is not just celebrating Sonic the Hedgehog's 35th anniversary; they're also throwing a major party for Puyo Puyo! The beloved puzzle game franchise will mark its milestone with the launch of the "Dairensa!" project, a nod to one of its iconic songs. This celebration promises a plethora of exciting events and initiatives, including a special live stream featuring voice actors and exclusive updates on the mobile game Puyo Puyo!! Quest.

From pop-up stores in major Japanese cities to a collaborative lottery featuring both Sonic and Puyo Puyo, SEGA is going all out. Fans can look forward to limited edition merchandise and themed events that will bring the community together. With so much planned, its clear that SEGA is committed to honoring its rich gaming legacy and engaging its audience in memorable ways.
