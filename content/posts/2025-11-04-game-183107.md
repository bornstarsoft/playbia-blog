In an exciting turn of events for gamers and Nintendo fans alike, both Mario Kart World and Donkey Kong Bananza have been shortlisted for the prestigious 'Console Game of the Year' at the Golden Joystick Awards. This recognition not only highlights the incredible lineup of the newly launched Switch 2 but also showcases the enduring popularity and innovation of Nintendo's flagship franchises.

With Mario Kart World competing for 'Best Multiplayer' and Donkey Kong Bananza nominated for 'Best Audio Design', these titles are up against some stiff competition, including heavyweights like Death Stranding 2 and Ghost of Ytei. The anticipation is palpable as fans eagerly await the results, which will be revealed on November 21, 2025.

As we look forward to the award announcements, it's clear that Nintendo continues to push the boundaries of gaming, offering experiences that resonate with both new players and long-time fans. Will these beloved titles take home the coveted awards? Only time will tell!
