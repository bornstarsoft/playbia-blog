The excitement is building as the beloved multiplayer party game, Fruit Mountain Party, is set to launch on Switch 2 and Switch in Spring 2026. Currently available in Early Access on PC, this vibrant 3D puzzle game has captured the hearts of players with its unique fruit-combining mechanics and competitive gameplay. The announcement promises to bring this fun-filled experience to a wider audience, allowing Switch users to join in on the fruity battles.

Players can look forward to a variety of game modes, including heated online battles and a single-player mode that supports global rankings. Whether youre stacking fruits with friends or competing for the highest score, Fruit Mountain Party is designed to be easy to pick up yet challenging to master. The game will be showcased at major gaming events, including Osaka Game Dungeon and Tokyo eSports Festa, giving fans a sneak peek at whats to come.
