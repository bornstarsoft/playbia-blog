This bold move backfired, contributing to the Celtics' historic collapse after holding a 3-1 series lead.

While Jaylen Brown and Derrick White shone with a combined 59 points, the lack of production from the starting trio left the Celtics in a deep hole early in the game. The decision to deviate from a more traditional lineup raises questions about Mazzulla's strategy and the impact of player performance under pressure. As the Celtics look to regroup for next season, fans and analysts alike will be eager to see how this experience shapes their approach moving forward.
