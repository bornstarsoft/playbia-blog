In a significant move to enhance its global gaming presence, JetSynthesys has acquired the popular merge puzzle game EverMerge, which has amassed over $350 million in lifetime revenue. This acquisition marks a pivotal step in JetSynthesys' strategy to build a diverse gaming ecosystem across various genres and markets, particularly as it aims to penetrate high-growth regions like India. EverMerge's impressive performance and strong user engagement in Western markets provide a solid foundation for this expansion.

Rajan Navani, Chairman & MD of JetSynthesys, emphasized the importance of this acquisition in bridging global intellectual properties with local insights. The company plans to leverage its expertise in community engagement and user acquisition to resonate with new audiences, particularly in emerging markets. As JetSynthesys continues to invest in EverMerge, the focus will be on enhancing content and delivering fresh experiences that keep players engaged.

This acquisition not only reinforces JetSynthesys' commitment to its player community but also positions the company as a formidable player in the global gaming landscape. As the gaming industry evolves, it will be interesting to see how JetSynthesys integrates EverMerge into its broader strategy and what innovative experiences await players in the future.
