Get ready for a wild ride as the highly anticipated urban survival game, Good Luck, is set to launch on PC on April 8, 2026. Developed by the indie studio Happybara Games, this physics-based platformer promises a unique blend of chaotic gameplay and lighthearted humor. Players will navigate a city filled with absurd traps and unexpected hazards, all while racing to reach a meeting. With both single-player and online co-op modes for up to five players, Good Luck is designed to keep you on your toes.

What sets Good Luck apart is its unpredictable physics and the absence of checkpoints, making every moment a test of skill and patience. Whether you're dodging a rogue banana peel or trying to outsmart a trash can, the game guarantees a hilarious yet challenging experience. The creators have described it as a fusion of urban survival and the rage game subgenre, especially in co-op mode where chaos reigns supreme.

As we count down to the release, it's clear that Good Luck is shaping up to be a must-play for fans of quirky and challenging games. Will you be ready to tackle the urban jungle when it drops on Steam? Let's hope you have your best survival strategies in place!
