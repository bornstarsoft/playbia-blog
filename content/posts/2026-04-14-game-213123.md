Nintendo has officially marked the 25th anniversary of its beloved Animal Crossing series with a delightful surprise for players of New Horizons. As part of the celebration, players received a special in-game gifta commemorative Leaf Statuealong with a nostalgic letter that pays homage to the original Nintendo 64 version, Dbutsu no Mori. This thoughtful gesture not only reinforces the franchise's legacy but also connects players to its rich history.

Alongside this surprise, Nintendo has also released update 3.0.2, which includes bug fixes and enhancements to improve the overall gameplay experience. Earlier this year, New Horizons received a significant content update featuring the new Nintendo Switch 2 Edition, which boasts impressive enhancements like 4K resolution and expanded online capabilities. With these updates, Nintendo continues to engage its community and keep the spirit of Animal Crossing alive.
