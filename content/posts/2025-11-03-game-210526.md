If youre a fan of word games, youll want to check out Shuffalo, the latest daily puzzle sensation from The New Yorker. This innovative game challenges players to spell words from scrambled letters, but with a twist: each time you successfully form a word, an additional letter is added to the mix. This dynamic gameplay keeps you on your toes and makes for a unique and engaging experience.

Shuffalo not only tests your vocabulary but also your ability to think quickly and adapt to new challenges. As you progress, the increasing complexity of the puzzles can lead to a rewarding sense of accomplishment. Its a perfect way to sharpen your mind while enjoying a bit of fun during your day.
