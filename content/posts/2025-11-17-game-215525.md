As we approach the end of 2025, Metacritic has unveiled its list of the worst games of the year, with Build a Rocket Boy's troubled title, MindsEye, taking the dubious honor of being the lowest-rated game. Scoring a mere 37 on the Metascore, MindsEye has faced significant backlash due to its technical issues, reminiscent of the infamous launch of Cyberpunk 2077. This situation has led to PlayStation offering refunds, a rare move that underscores the game's poor reception.

Following MindsEye, the Nintendo Switch 2 mini-game collection, Tamagotchi Plaza, and the interactive experience Switch 2 Welcome Tour also found themselves on the list, with scores of 43 and 54, respectively. Critics have pointed out that these games suffer from simplistic gameplay and lack of innovation, raising questions about the quality control in game development today.

As gamers, we must reflect on what these low scores mean for the industry. Are developers prioritizing quantity over quality? With the gaming landscape constantly evolving, it will be interesting to see how these trends impact future releases and player expectations.
