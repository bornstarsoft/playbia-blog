The newly launched visual puzzle game AiliA, developed by IceToad Studio LLC and published by Gamirror Games, is making waves in the gaming community. Priced at $11.99 with a limited-time 20% discount, AiliA invites players to dive into a unique 3D world where mirrors are not just reflections but interactive elements that reshape the environment and the gameplay experience.

In AiliA, players step into the shoes of Aria, a young girl navigating through beautifully crafted stages that blend reality with a mirrored dimension. The game boasts over 100 puzzles, utilizing innovative mechanics involving water reflections and recursive mirror structures. Additionally, a special collaboration bundle with Moncage offers an extra 10% discount for puzzle enthusiasts looking to expand their gaming library.

For those curious about the game, a free demo is available on Steam, allowing players to explore the intertwining worlds of reality and reflection.
