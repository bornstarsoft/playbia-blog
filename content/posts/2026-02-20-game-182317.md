Fans of the iconic God of War series have reason to be excited as actor Christopher Judge, the voice of Kratos, hinted at a major announcement coming this summer. During a recent appearance at Fan Expo in Vancouver, Judge mentioned that while he wont be part of the upcoming God of War Trilogy Remake, he teased that we can expect news about what Santa Monica Studio is working on soon. This revelation has sparked speculation about the next big project from the acclaimed developer.

The God of War franchise has captivated audiences with its rich storytelling and innovative gameplay, and with the success of 2022s God of War Ragnarok, anticipation is at an all-time high. Judges comments suggest that fans may soon be treated to a new chapter in Kratos' journey, potentially featuring a fresh fighting system as hinted in the remake. As we await more details, the gaming community is left to wonder what direction the beloved series will take next.
