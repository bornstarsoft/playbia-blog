Capcom is gearing up for an exciting Resident Evil Showcase this week, set to unveil new gameplay footage from the highly anticipated Resident Evil Requiem. Scheduled for January 15 at 2 PM PT, this showcase promises to deliver fresh insights into the game, which is set to release on February 27, 2026. Fans can tune in via the Resident Evil YouTube channel or Twitch to catch all the action live.

The recent teaser footage highlighted the stunning Path Tracing visuals powered by Nvidia graphics cards, offering a glimpse into the eerie environments players will navigate, including the fictional city of Wrenwood. With the addition of iconic characters like Leon, the game is expected to blend stealth and action, creating a unique gameplay experience that could appeal to a wide range of players.
